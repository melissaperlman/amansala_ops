Dietary & Allergy Information
What we need to know — and what we don't:
Our kitchen is already dairy-light, pork-free, and gluten-conscious — we do not serve red meat, pork, or heavy gluten. You do not need to tell us about lactose intolerance, gluten sensitivity, or beef/pork avoidance.

Please only flag: true severe allergies (nuts, shellfish, soy, eggs, etc.) and vegetarians or vegans, so our kitchen can plan portions and protein alternatives.
⚠️ Important: Amansala will do our best to accommodate all severe allergies, however we are not a nut-free or allergen-free facility. Any guest with a life-threatening allergy must carry their own epi-pen at all times, including during all activities, tours, and off-site excursions. Please confirm this with your group before arrival.
What does our kitchen need to know?
✓ Please tell us
Vegetarians — we'll plan plant-based protein
Vegans — fully plant-based, no eggs or dairy
Severe nut allergy (e.g. peanuts, tree nuts)
Severe shellfish allergy
Severe soy, egg, or other life-threatening allergy
✗ No need to mention
Dairy / lactose — our kitchen is already dairy-light
Gluten sensitivity — we prepare gluten-conscious meals
Red meat / beef / pork — we do not serve these
Mild food preferences — e.g. "doesn't like spicy"
⚠ Epi-pen policy
Amansala is not an allergen-free facility. We accommodate all severe allergies with care, but cross-contamination cannot be fully guaranteed. Any guest with a life-threatening allergy must carry their own epi-pen at all times.
